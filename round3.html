Round 3

Rules

  • You will be asked to list a certain topic.
  • Each question will have a large number of possible answers.
  • You will take turns providing one answer to the question.
  • You are allowed with one "pass" to skip your turn if you can't answer within the allotted time.
  • Each turn is limited to 5 seconds.
  • If you fail to provide an answer within the allocated time, whether or not you've used your pass, you will be eliminated from the round.
  • The winner of this round earns 5 points.
